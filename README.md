# Vampire Survivor? (GGJ 2026)

## Theme

Masks

## Description

Simple game where you look for a vampire amongst a crowd of masqueraders. The vampire layer has a bunch of floors in his lair and can hide behind his mask to avoid detection. You can interrogate individuals to find out if they are the vampire or not, but in the meantime the vampire is free to move around and bite other visitor, either killing them or turning them into vampires too.

2026 submission to the Global Game Jam hosted in Guatemala at UVG. Made with frijolitos and Godot 4
